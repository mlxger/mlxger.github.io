About Me · 关于我

I am currently a master's student in Cybersecurity at Fujian Normal University. I am simultaneously working as an AI Algorithm Engineer at Fuzhou Lianhe Xiaozhi Information Technology Co., Ltd., focused on AI engineering and applied innovation.
